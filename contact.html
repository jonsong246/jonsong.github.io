Hello! My name is Jonathan Song and I recently graduated with a Bachelors in 3D Animation at Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, New York. I am originally from Orange County, California and I have moved to the San Francisco to further pursue a career in software development in hopes to use both fields of study to create more complex projects in the future. Here you will find a collection of my work primarily constructed through various software including Autodesk Maya, Adobe Premiere, Photoshop, and Aftereffects. These are a few of my projects throughout the course of my academic years at Pratt. Through the creation of this webpage, I am able to showcase my work and hope to deliver some insight as well as receive constructive feedback. Thank you for visiting my page!
